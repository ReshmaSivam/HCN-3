FAQs

What is Front-end Mentor, and how will it help me ?

Frontend Mentor offers realistic coding challenges to help developers improve their frontend coding skills with projects in HTML, CSS and JavaScript. It's suitable for all levels and ideal for portfolio building.

Is Frontend Mentor free ?

Yes!, Frontend mentor is completely free for all the Developers/Users/Students.

Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio ?

Yes!, Absolutely you can use the projects that you developed in Frontend Mentor in your Portfolio.

How can I get help if I'm stuck on a challenge ?

You don't have to worry about errors or doubts. Join Our Frontend Mentor Community in where you can get a help from your co-developers/users.
